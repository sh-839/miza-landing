1. Introduction

MIZA LLC ("Company," "we," "us," or "our") operates the MIZA mobile application and website (collectively, the "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information through our Service.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our Service. This Privacy Policy applies to all users of MIZA, regardless of location. If you do not agree with our privacy practices, please do not use our Service.

By using MIZA, you consent to this Privacy Policy. If you are under 13 years old, your parent or legal guardian must consent to this policy on your behalf.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address and username
  • Profile Information: Display name, location (optional), and genre preferences
  • Video Content: Video clips you upload to the Service, including metadata such as upload date, duration, and descriptions
  • Communications: Messages sent through our support channels, including customer inquiries and feedback

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: Operating system, device type, device identifiers, and app version
  • Usage Data: Features accessed, search queries, videos viewed, interaction patterns, and session duration
  • Crash Reports: Error logs and diagnostic information to improve service stability
  • IP Address: Used to determine location for fraud prevention and analytics
  • Cookies and Similar Technologies: When using our website, we use cookies to maintain sessions and preferences

2.3 Information from Third Parties

  • OAuth Providers: When you sign up using Apple or Google OAuth, we receive your email, name, and profile picture
  • Service Providers: Our third-party providers may share information with us for transaction processing and fraud prevention

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To create and maintain your account
  • To authenticate your identity and verify your age (minimum 13 years)
  • To store, process, and deliver your video content
  • To provide customer support and respond to your inquiries
  • To detect, prevent, and address fraud, abuse, and security incidents
  • To personalize your experience and display content based on your preferences
  • To analyze usage patterns and improve our Service
  • To send you service-related announcements and updates
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Service
  • To conduct research and develop new features
  • To market our Service (with your consent where required)

4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

For users in the European Union and other jurisdictions with similar laws, we process your personal data based on:

  • Contractual Necessity: Processing needed to provide our Service
  • Legal Obligation: Compliance with applicable laws (e.g., child safety, DMCA)
  • Legitimate Interests: Fraud prevention, security, and service improvement
  • Consent: For marketing communications and optional features

6. Data Retention

  • Account Data: Retained while your account is active. Deleted within 30 days of account deletion, unless required by law
  • Video Content: Retained until you delete it or your account is deleted. After deletion, videos are purged within 30 days
  • Usage Logs: Retained for 90 days for analytics and security purposes
  • Crash Reports: Retained for 30 days and then anonymized
  • Legal Holds: If we receive legal process, we may retain data longer as required by law

7. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption of data in transit using TLS/SSL protocols
  • Encryption of video content at rest in cloud storage
  • Password hashing and salting to prevent unauthorized access
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Limited access to personal data on a need-to-know basis
  • Mandatory confidentiality agreements with all service providers

Important: No security measure is completely secure. While we use reasonable efforts to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security. You are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your account credentials.
